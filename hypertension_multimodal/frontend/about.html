About HyperRNet

HyperRNet is a multimodal artificial intelligence system designed to estimate hypertension risk using both clinical health indicators and retinal fundus imaging.

Why Hypertension Prediction Matters

Hypertension remains one of the leading causes of cardiovascular disease worldwide. Early detection plays a critical role in preventing complications such as stroke, heart failure, and kidney disease.

Traditional screening methods rely primarily on clinical measurements. However, recent research has shown that retinal fundus images contain valuable microvascular information that can reveal cardiovascular risk factors.

How the Platform Works

1. Clinical Input

Users provide structured clinical information such as blood pressure, BMI, age, smoking status, and diabetes indicators.

2. Retinal Image Analysis

A deep learning model analyzes retinal fundus images to identify vascular patterns associated with hypertension risk.

3. Multimodal Fusion

Predictions from both models are combined using a late fusion strategy to produce a final hypertension risk estimate.

System Architecture

Clinical Model

Machine learning model trained on structured health records.

Retinal Model

Deep learning model based on ResNet-50 architecture.

Fusion Model

Weighted late fusion combining clinical and retinal predictions.
